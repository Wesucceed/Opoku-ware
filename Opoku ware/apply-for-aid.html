Financial aid application process

  1. Complete the Opoku Ware Scholarship Profile
    Follow the following steps to complete the profile:
    • Contact Opoku Ware Financial Aid Office and request for the Opoku Ware
      Scholarship Profile forms from the financial aid office.
    • Fill the forms.
    • Add two passport pictures to the final submission of the forms.
    • Submit the filled forms and the two passport pictures.
  2. Request for financial aid interview via email ( owass-finaid@gmail.com)
  3. If applicant gains the full financial aid, it is required that the applicant send a
    letter of thanksgiving to the financial aid office.

If applicant needs more information about the financial aid application, contact the financial aid
office via email owass-finaid@gmail.com.

Frequently asked questions about financial aid process:

If I receive financial aid as a new student, am I guaranteed financial aid during my entire time
at Opoku Ware?
All financial aid applicants must reapply for financial aid each year. If your family’s economic
situation remains constant, you will receive a financial aid award that is comparable to the award that
you received your first year.
If I don’t receive financial aid when I am admitted, can I apply again once I am enrolled?
Applicants who do not apply for financial aid when they apply for admission (and who are admitted) will
be eligible to apply for financial aid at any time after enrolling as a student at Opoku Ware School.
Is any student eligible for financial aid?
Applicants who can provide at most 1000 dollars per year towards their education are eligible.
Applicants who can provide more than 1000 dollars per year towards their education are not eligible.

Financial Aid application Deadlines

Please pay close attention to the deadlines. Late applications may not be considered.
Spring Transfer - July 2 each year.
Early Decision: November 1 each year.
Regular Decision: January 2 each year.
Fall Transfer: March 15 each year.
