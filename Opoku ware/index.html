About Opoku Ware School

Opoku Ware School is an all boys school that aim at promoting science and math by educating s tudents interested in the field of science and math. It is renowned for its numerous resources such as science labs, IT labs, math and science libraries that support the male child education and science and math education. Through science and math resources that the school provides with the students, the current students and past students of the school have excelled in the fields of STEM. Not only is the school known for its support for science and math education, but it also known for its generosity in providing financial aid for low-income s tudents. It has a record of providing full financial aid to meet the full demonstrated need of low-income students through its well-known Opoku Ware Scholar's Program.
