Types of Financial Aid

We understand that everyone’s financial situation is unique and there are no one-size-fits-all solutions. The family profiles below offer examples of real financial aid packages recently offered to Opoku Ware students. They do not address complex issues such as owning a family business. Each applicant’s financial aid package is dependent on multiple factors, and you may receive more or less aid than shown in the examples. All families are encouraged to start by using the Financial Aid Calculator, an online tool that will provide you with estimated eligibility for financial aid from Opoku Ware School.

Opoku Ware Scholar's Program

It can be extremely challenging for even the brightest,most resilient students who needs financial aid. Opoku Ware Scholar's Program at Opok Ware School provides four years support to exceptional students of Opoku Ware School— helping them achieve their dreams through academic, financial, and social support. Alongside Opoku Ware professional staff committed to their success, Opoku Ware Scholars discover how to be their best and explore science and math resources that can help them shape their future.

It is the only financial aid program that can support low-income students. All students who are in dire need of financial aid( can provide at most 1000 dollars per year towards their education) are eligible to apply for the Opoku Ware Scholar's Program. Though competitive to get the scholarship, when gotten, the Opoku Ware Scholar's Program provides full financial support towards the education of the scholar.

The impact of Opoku Ware Scholar:

  • 92% of the scholars say their experience is good.
  • 91% of the scholars got into top-tier schools in the world.
  • 80% of the scholars got the chance to engage in science and
    math extracurricular activites like interships, quizzes, and research.

Work

Regardless of your eligibility for financial aid, Opoku Ware offers a wide variety of part-time jobs.
Working a modest number of hours each week provides you with resources to help make Opoku Ware affordable.
Find out what your employment earnings expectation should be for a job at Opoku Ware.

Employment

You can work as a student employee as long as you remain registered full-time in a diploma-granting program,
enrolled in classes, and have no registration, or other holds on your Opoku Ware School record.
Opoku Ware School does not place students in jobs. You conduct your own job search to identify opportunities that
best fit your interests, skills, and class schedules. Our student employment job database posts on-campus
student job opportunities, and part-time jobs in the local Santasi area.
